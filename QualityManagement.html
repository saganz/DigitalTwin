BALANCE STANDARDISATION AND FLEXIBILITY 

Now more than ever, your reputation hinges on the quality of your product or service. Prove your excellence with certifications that customers trust — like ISO 9001. In regulated environments, such as finance, pharmaceuticals and health care — quality management plays a critical role in ensuring products and services are consistently high in quality, safe and ready for market. With your organization undergoing a digital transformation that impacts every operation across your business, old tools and tech aren’t good enough anymore. Rethinking your quality management tools is what’s needed. Do this by creating an Enterprise Digital Twin with help from Software AG. Your Enterprise Digital Twin visualizes your organization and offers a consistent view of all interdependencies related to quality management. Put your organization under the microscope of a digital twin to see what’s happening now — and more importantly — to identify the changes required for the future. Transform into a quality-driven organization!

Unleash the power of quality

While consolidating your different Quality Management Systems (QMS) into one standardized system, you’ll find many opportunities to improve quality on your way. Your Quality Management Digital Twin, establishes a single source of truth and will be valuable to everyone in the company and especially to those who are in charge of the quality-driven business. Standardization will drive excellence.

Communicate, collaborate & govern

The perfect foundation to build your central QMS is your Enterprise Digital Twin. Its primarly objectives will be to share and collaborate on business processes, documents and systems available to all employees and auditors worldwide.
With a central, easy-to-use QMS, everyone in the company can join the global quality excellence initiative. Not only join the community and get the right information! The power of the Quality Management Digital Twin lies in people easily pushing change and transformation. Users can give feedback or request changes request to drive continuous improvement — because achieving quality is a never-ending commitment.

Address end-users exactly the way they need

Simplified access to information is an accelerator for the business. The Quality Management digital twin is consistent, open to everyone and provides the ideal information to your organization so your team can perform their daily jobs. How? Your Quality Management Digital Twin offers a simple, attractive and user-friendly way to explore information which accelerates business and is key for end-user acceptance. The Quality Management Digital Twin offers role-based access to information which is presented in different ways according to the information type and end user.  Evaluate process quality and acceptance based on the number of visits and views of a process description or the number of change requests.
