RISK & COMPLIANCE DIGITAL TWIN:
YOUR LENS INTO THE BUSINESS

The significance of compliance and risk management is steadily increasing in our hyper-connected world. Exposure to legal penalties, financial forfeiture and material loss has never been greater — if you fail to act in accordance with industry laws and regulations, internal policies or prescribed best practices. With regulatory requirements becoming more stringent, like the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), compliance requires precise knowledge of the data you own and the right data management policies across your organization. Create a Risk & Compliance Management Digital Twin to help you meet both internal and external regulatory requirements, manage business risks and turn risk and compliance management into a strategic management tool.

Identify and eliminate threats

Threats and risks to your business impact everything from revenue to morale of your employees. Fraud and financial hacks can damage your reputation and your future. How can you stop it before it starts? Using ARIS to build your Digital Twin for Risk & Compliance Management you can easily identify inefficient controls, put new ones in place and thus eliminate risks. It also can help you dive deep into the unknowns. With a mirror image of your organizations assets, policies and risk and control landscape — your Risk & Compliance Digital Twin can help you visualize and secure your organization from end to end.


Secure success of improvement initiatives

Your Risk & Compliance Digital Twin is the ideal platform to create corporate improvement initiatives. Ranging from the identification of internal measures and controls, to easy documentation of control execution and the establishment of a regular schedule supported by task management-based workflows, your digital twin ensures compliance, effectiveness assessment and secures your reports to the respective authorities. Tight controls over process changes are secured by detailed workflows. They are clearly assigned to roles and responsibilities, automatically notify of on new tasks and reminders before due date and support the four-eyes principle and audit-proof documentation of results.

Increase engagement and mitigate risks

Stakeholders will be enthusiastic about risk mitigation when they view the details via heat maps and dashboards. It helps to evaluate assessment results and check if the measures are still appropriate or need an update. Incidents can be documented, and affected organizational units, processes, cost centers and IT systems can easily be identified.
Auditing efforts are reduced and audits are performed with confidence and minimal work
based on well thought-out plans. Responsibilities are clearly defined and a clear audit trail is created. Single audit steps and their progress can be visualized on a time axis using Gantt charts.
