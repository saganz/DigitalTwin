Digital Twin

A digital twin is a representation of your physical assets. Digital twins are being introduced across all lines of business as an “Enterprise Digital Twin” — one that illustrates your business operating model from end-to-end. Many organizations are using it to coordinate the critical interdependencies between people, processes and IT within complex digital business transformations.
