ADD TRANSPARENCY AND RESPONSIVENESS WITH YOUR SUPPLY CHAIN DIGITAL TWIN

In our consumer-driven demanding world of e-commerce, your supply chain is always on, always connected, always creating information. The
Internet of Things (IoT) has made it possible to track and trace virtually anything within the supply chain — and it has changed the game for supply chain operators. To be successful and to optimize the supply chain, many industry leading companies are creating an Enterprise  Digital Twin of their business. An Enterprise Digital Twin is a virtual replica of all the processes and business information within your organization. As your business changes, use your “twin” to monitor your processes in real-time or take a specific action. Your Enterprise Digital Twin creates a continuous loop that allows you to quickly and continuously fine-tune your supply chain network.

LEARN MORE

Be effective and efficient

Easily find information that shows the supply chain facts and figures to identify potential risks and problems.Using a supply chain management dashboard you can see an overview of your current situation. Drill down into KPIs of individual processes — like procurement, delivery and logistics processes from a high-level perspective, checking for a solution and analyzing process exceptions and faults.

Respond in real-time

Analyse your current situation with logistics providers and assess suppliers in order to optimize the processes. That means looking at the as-is processes to find bottlenecks and optimize them. To check if the processes corresponds to the reality compare the actual (as-is) and desired (to-be) processes side-by-side and identify differences.

Take it to your eco-system

When your processes need re-work, leverage your Supply Chain Digital Twin to simulate different scenarios to determine process alternatives so you can decide how to proceed with the scenario planning. With a plan in place, you can quickly on-board the entire organization by taking it to be implemented through your Center of Excellence.
